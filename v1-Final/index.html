Accident & insurance claims

We're an experienced workshop for accident repairs and insurance claims. Bring the documents in the checklist and our team will guide you through the process from start to finish.

Start your claim on WhatsApp Call 014-232 1939

Documents Checklist

  • Police Report
  • Owner's NRIC & License
  • Driver's NRIC & License
  • Vehicle Ownership Certificate (VOC)
  • Insurance Policy / Cover Note
  • Accident Scene / Police Photos
  • Business Registration (company vehicles)
  • GDL / PSV License (commercial vehicles)

Not sure which apply to you? Just WhatsApp us a photo of your documents and we'll check.

How to find us

We're just off the main road near the flyover. As you approach the traffic-light junction, watch for our yellow "Wayson Auto" signboard and turn onto the service road. It's easy to miss if you stay on the main road.

  • Turn off at the junction by the flyover, following the yellow signboard.
  • Don't continue straight on the main road past the junction.
